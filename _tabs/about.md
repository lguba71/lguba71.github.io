I am a freelance mechanical design engineer helping manufacturing companies, machine builders, and hardware teams turn mechanical ideas into manufacturable CAD models, production drawings, and technical documentation.

My work is focused on practical mechanical design: parts that can be manufactured, assemblies that are not unnecessarily complicated, and documentation that production teams can actually use.

## What I can help with

- 3D CAD modeling
- machine parts
- fixtures and jigs
- manufacturing drawings
- design-for-manufacturing improvements
- BOM preparation
- technical documentation
- CAD model cleanup and redesign

## CAD tools

- Onshape
- PTC Creo
- Autodesk Inventor

## Engineering approach

I prefer simple, robust mechanical solutions over unnecessary complexity.

My design work is focused on:

- reducing part count where possible
- improving manufacturability
- simplifying assembly
- improving reliability
- creating clear documentation for manufacturing

## Typical deliverables

- CAD models
- production drawings
- design notes
- BOMs
- DFM feedback

## Availability

Available for freelance mechanical design projects.
